About


A Panchayat is a village level institution in India that has been vested with the authority to impose taxes. It consists of a group of five or more representatives, who are responsible for the governance of the village. The panchayat is empowerd to collect taxes on land and property, as well as on professional and commercial activities within the village.


The purpose of a panchayat is to provide basic services to villages such as water, sanitation, roads and primary health.The panchayat also imposes taxes on villagers to raise funds for these services.


This website helps the user to know all the information about the various taxes of the gram panchayat. For this the user have to register initially and then they can login into the website to pay and to know the status of different taxes.
